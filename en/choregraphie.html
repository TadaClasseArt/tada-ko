Choreography

Tadayoshi, dancer and choreographer, draws his inspiration from history, philosophy and the social sciences. He places the human being at the centre of his creations, blending classical and contemporary dance for existing companies or as a freelancer. He also composes his own soundtracks, extending the world of his works.
